# Product Overview

## Product Purpose

FormForge AI is an advanced, browser-based PDF editor designed to streamline the creation and management of interactive PDF forms. By integrating Google's Gemini AI, it simplifies the process of identifying form fields and enhancing document interactivity, solving the problem of manual, tedious PDF form creation.

## Target Users

- **Administrative Professionals**: Who need to digitize paper forms or create fillable PDFs for organizational use.
- **Developers & Designers**: Looking for a customizable tool to prototype or build complex form layouts.
- **End Users**: Individuals who need to fill out or sign PDF documents quickly and efficiently.

## Key Features

1. **Interactive Form Builder**: Drag-and-drop support for various form controls including Text Fields, Checkboxes, Radio Buttons, Dropdowns, Signatures, and Freehand Ink.
2. **AI-Powered Field Detection**: Utilizes Google Gemini AI to automatically analyze PDF content and suggest interactive form fields.
3. **Comprehensive PDF Editing**: Full-featured workspace with zoom controls, panning, and property panels for fine-tuning control appearance and behavior.
4. **Multi-Language Support**: Built-in localization for multiple languages (English, Chinese, Spanish, French, German, Japanese).
5. **Modern UI/UX**: A responsive, accessible interface built with Radix UI and Tailwind CSS, featuring dark/light mode support.

## Business Objectives

- **Efficiency**: Reduce the time required to convert static PDFs into interactive forms.
- **Accessibility**: Make PDF editing tools accessible to users without specialized software installation.
- **Innovation**: Leverage AI to provide smart features that distinguish the product from traditional PDF editors.

## Success Metrics

- **User Engagement**: Number of PDFs uploaded and forms created.
- **AI Utilization**: Frequency of AI detection feature usage and acceptance rate of suggestions.
- **Performance**: Application load times and rendering speed for large PDF files.

## Product Principles

1. **Simplicity First**: The interface should be intuitive, hiding complexity until needed (e.g., via property panels).
2. **Local-First Processing**: Prioritize client-side processing for PDF rendering and editing to ensure speed and privacy.
3. **AI as Assistant**: AI features should assist and suggest, but the user always retains final control.

## Future Vision

To become the go-to web-based solution for intelligent document processing and form management.

### Potential Enhancements

- **Cloud Storage Integration**: Direct saving/loading from Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.
- **Collaborative Editing**: Real-time multi-user editing capabilities.
- **Advanced AI**: Form filling automation and content extraction/summarization.
